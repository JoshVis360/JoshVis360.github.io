5 Ways to Keep Your Car Running Better

  • 1. Stick to Regular Oil Changes
    Keep your engine lubricated and cool. Change oil every 5,000–10,000 km to avoid sludge and costly repairs.
  • 2. Don’t Ignore Warning Lights
    Check engine lights are early warnings—diagnose them before they become expensive problems.
  • 3. Keep Tyres Properly Inflated
    Proper tyre pressure improves fuel efficiency and extends tyre life. Check monthly!
  • 4. Watch Your Driving Habits
    Smooth driving reduces wear on your engine, brakes, and suspension. Ease up on the pedal!
  • 5. Stick to a Service Schedule
    Routine maintenance helps catch issues early and keeps your car running strong and safe.
