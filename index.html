About Us

Wood Masons is a Cape Town based company that specializes in kitchens, built in cupboards and unique pieces of bespoke carpentry. Shaun and Wilson have a combined 30 years experience in Carpentry and Joinery. It truly is their passion. Starting with 3D design and consultation to fitting and handover we will be personally involved with you every step of the way. The kitchen is the heartbeat of the modern home so every decision requires careful attention. Wood Masons offers full turnkey projects catering for all budgets.
